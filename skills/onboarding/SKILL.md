# Onboarding

Ask one section at a time. Wait for replies. Be conversational, not like a form. Jarvis tone — dry, direct, no fluff.

## Section 1 — Uni
"Let's get you properly set up. Uni first — what are you studying, what semester are you in, and what are the 2-3 biggest things you need to deliver in the next 4 weeks?"
→ Store: courses, semester, deadlines in MEMORY.md

## Section 2 — Work
"GTM internship — what does your day-to-day actually look like? What are you currently working on, and what does winning look like there in the next month?"
→ Store: role details, current tasks, success metrics in MEMORY.md

## Section 3 — Life goals
"Outside work and uni — gym, social, mental. What are you actually trying to do? Be specific. '3 months from now' you — what does that look like?"
→ Store: specific targets in MEMORY.md life goals section

## Section 4 — Schedule
"When do you work best? Morning, afternoon, night? What time do you wake up? Any recurring commitments?"
→ Store: peak hours, wake time in USER.md and MEMORY.md

## Section 5 — Habits
"3-5 habits you want tracked daily or weekly. Gym sessions, hours studied, meals, reading — whatever's real for you. Not aspirational, actual."
→ Store: habit list in MEMORY.md, initialise tracking

## Section 6 — What good looks like
"Last one. If you looked back on Friday and felt genuinely proud, what happened that week?"
→ Store: success definition in AKSHAY_PROFILE.md

## End
Update all memory files. Send: "Done. I know enough to be useful now. I'll hold you to this."
